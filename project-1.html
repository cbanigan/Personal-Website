Research

Research project underneath Dr. Chu within The StoryLab at Texas A&M. I created an Android Wear application to help elementary students understand science concepts through informal science learning on wearable devices. After creating a base version, we also created two separate versions with gameification elements to test whether storyified interfaces or gameified interfaces of the same application lead to more overall positive engagement.
